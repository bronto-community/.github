# 🦕 Bronto Community

**Projects built by the Bronto team and community — for everyone who cares about observability.**

Welcome! This is the community home of [Bronto](https://bronto.io), the intelligent observability data platform. Everything here is unofficial, experimental, or just plain fun — built by Brontos and friends, and shared in the open because someone else might find it useful too.

> Looking for Bronto's official open source projects, integrations, and developer tooling? Head over to **[github.com/brontoio](https://github.com/brontoio)**.

---

## 🧭 What you'll find here

This org is intentionally broad. Projects range from polished tools to weekend experiments:

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| 📚 **Tutorials & guides** | Hands-on walkthroughs, sample setups, learning material |
| ✍️ **Blog posts & collateral** | Drafts, companion code, talk material, demos |
| 🤖 **Agents & automation** | Internal AI agents and workflows we're able to share |
| 🦕 **Bronto-specific projects** | Tools, extensions, and experiments built around Bronto |
| 🔭 **Observability at large** | Broader logging, metrics, tracing, and OpenTelemetry projects |
| 🧪 **Everything else** | If it's useful and shareable, it belongs here |

## 🤝 Contributing

We'd love your input, whether you're on the Bronto team or not:

- **Found a bug or have an idea?** Open an issue on the relevant repo.
- **Want to improve something?** Pull requests are welcome everywhere.
- **Built something that fits here?** Open an issue on any repo (or reach out) and let's talk about bringing it in.

Each project documents its own setup and contribution details in its README.

## ⚠️ A quick note

Projects in this org are **community-maintained and not officially supported by Bronto**. They come with no SLAs and may move fast, break things, or be gloriously unfinished. For supported integrations and tooling, see [github.com/brontoio](https://github.com/brontoio).
